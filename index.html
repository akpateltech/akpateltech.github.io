Engineering • Product • Reliability

Building scalable systems, leading impactful teams, and solving complex engineering challenges.

I am a software engineer and an engineering leader passionate about building reliable, scalable systems that solve real business problems. Most recently, I was a Senior Software Engineer (Tech Lead) at Oleria, leading the development of adaptive identity security solutions that improved access management and reduced identity risks across cloud environments.

Before that, I spent over three years at Atlassian, where I piloted the GA launch of Atlassian Analytics for 25,000+ enterprise clients, architected a low-latency, 99.99% SLA event sourcing pipeline, and cut system alerts by 60% through better observability and tooling.

At Bounce, I led the payments vertical, integrating multiple payment gateways and achieving a 99.9% transaction success rate, while also automating KYC with a 70% success rate. At Paytm, I built products including Bus Cancellation Insurance and Paytm Gold—cutting fraud by 30%, boosting revenue by 15%, and earning the Rising Star Award in 2017.

Earlier in my career, I worked at eBay, where I boosted v4 server throughput by 250% and played a key role in decoupling systems during the eBay-PayPal split, and at Samsung R&D, where I developed the Backup and Restore app used across Galaxy and Note devices.

I hold a B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering from MANIT Bhopal, and over the last 12+ years, I’ve scaled backend services, designed resilient architectures, and mentored teams to deliver with speed and quality. I continue to dive deep into engineering challenges and share my learnings through writing, talks, and mentoring.
